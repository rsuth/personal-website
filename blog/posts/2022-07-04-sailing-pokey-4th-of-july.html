Sailing Pokey: 4th of July

2022-07-04

We set sail around 7:40pm to see the Big Bay Boom fireworks show. We probably should have left earlier - We weren't even past the Coronado bridge by the time the show started! Still had some great views.

Getting back to the dock was not very fun - lots of motorboats in a hurry to get back makes a slow and bumpy ride.
