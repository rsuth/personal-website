Sailing Pokey: San Diego Bay

2021-08-19

Went for a nice sail today around San Diego Bay. Winds were 10kts steady from the west, with gusts to 15kts. Great time practicing sail trim "racing" every other sailboat we saw. We got going pretty fast, sometimes heeling 30 degrees or more.
